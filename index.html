About Us

"The love for baking and cooking snuck up on me. It started with me having a family and wanting better for them than the preservative filled offerings in the stores. They would come home with prepackaged cakes/snacks so I started baking at home and they loved it. As they got older I included them in the fun. Sunday dinner became a tradition. I’d make a meal that was too time consuming for a weekday and some sort of baked good for afterwards. I discovered I loved to please people with a delicious plate so I went to culinary school and was certified in Pastry Arts. I hope you enjoy my offerings as much as I enjoy making them."
- PJ
